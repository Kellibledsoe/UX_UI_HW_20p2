Problem Statement

The Buddy Cruise website was designed to achieve providing educating opportunities and resources for families while promoting awareness, advocacy, and inclusion for individuals with special needs. We have observed that our service isn't meeting these goals, which is causing an adverse effect to our business. How might we improve our Buddy Cruise website so that our customers are successful based on our web flow?
